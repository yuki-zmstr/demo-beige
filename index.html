01    About Me

Hi there! My name is Yukihide.

I am a Singapore-Japan mix currently studying in the National University of Singapore, pursuing a Bachelor of Engineering in Industrial and Systems Engineering.

I spent my years growing up in three different countries - Singapore, Taiwan, and Japan, each for about 6 years. After serving National Service in Singapore for two years, I matriculated to NUS in August 2020.

Think, plan, execute, reflect. I learn best through this iterative process. Being an active learner, I am open to various kinds of work experience where I can hone both hard and soft skills.

Recently, I have been exploring the field of Machine Learning; I have implemented and tested a few regression, classification, clustering, and ANN models myself, and I hope to learn more about the potentials of data utilization.

Feel free to reach out!
