About Me

Hello, my name is Chun Kiat. I am a Singaporean currently working in Tokyo, Japan. I graduated from the National University of Singapore in 2016 with a Bachelor of Business Administration. Thereafter, I moved to Japan to begin my life as a system engineer working in the Fintech industry. I like travelling, dancing and I fell in love with coding in the summer of 2017.

Being a dreamer with far-fetched technological ideas, I am interested in many fields including Fintech, future transport, renewable energy, reverse aging, space travel and the list goes on. I enjoy intellectual conversations on such topics.

With nearly three years of experience working on financial systems, I am relatively well-versed in the implementation of robust and high-availability systems, on both cloud technologies and dedicated servers. In addition, I am constantly improving my skills through personal projects that include small web applications as well as system applications.

Feel free to drop me any questions about living and working in Japan.
