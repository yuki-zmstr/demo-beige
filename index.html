01   About Me

Hi there! My name is Yukihide.

I am a Singapore-Japan mix currently enrolled at the National University of Singapore, pursuing a Bachelor of Engineering under Industrial and Systems Engineering.

I spent my years growing up in three different countries - Singapore, Taiwan, and Japan, each for about six years. After serving National Service in Singapore for two years, I matriculated to NUS in August 2020.

Plan, execute, reflect, improve. I learn best through this iterative process. Being an active learner, I am open to various kinds of experiences where I can hone both hard and soft skills.

I've recently been exploring the field of Machine Learning. It's truly amazing how a well-trained model can assist in decision making, from predicting numerical values to assigning classifications, and also how easy it is to build such a model with open-source code.

I am fluent in English and Japanese, as well as conversational Chinese.

Please feel free to reach out!
